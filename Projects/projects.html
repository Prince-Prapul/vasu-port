Medal Collection Software

An application made for the purpose of cataloging medals, awards and decorations of various kinds. As most available applications for this purpose are either old or clunky, the intention was to create a sleek and stylish application for phalerists while avoiding any compromise on features.
The application itself is built in Angular on the Electron framework to ensure maximum customizability.
It's features include the ability to store, categorize, and label medals, and it also allows transfer of medals between two machines. The application also maintains a record of medals added and removed and their valuations.
