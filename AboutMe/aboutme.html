About Me
I'm an Engineer, History Buff, Web Developer, Poet, Guitarist, and I absolutely adore cats!

An Introduction

I can regularly be found working on some coding project late into the night, reading a slow-burn romance novel, obsessing over lines of poetry and song lyrics with my best friend or trying to get a cat to sit on my lap.

I am an dreamer at heart and and I crave the thrill of travelling and wandering, to places unexplored, to times long past or to simply escape into a fantasy world. As Tolkien put it, "Fantasy is escapist, and that is its glory. If a soldier is imprisioned by the enemy, don’t we consider it his duty to escape?. . .If we value the freedom of mind and soul, if we’re partisans of liberty, then it’s our plain duty to escape, and to take as many people with us as we can!"

Hobbies

Music

I'm an aspiring guitarist and singer, and I play for the band Vajra, you could find some of our work on YouTube or Instagram, and if you live near Mumbai, maybe hear us perform live as well! As for listening, I basically listen to all kinds of music, if you want, you can follow me on Spotify and share a few songs!

Literature

I love reading! I'm extremely into thrillers and classics, and I'm always up for long discussions on a story or a character, regardless of where they're from. I'll also be linking a reading list soon!

History

I'm really into history, especially the period of Classical Antiquity. One of my favourite pastimes is just to think about laws and their effect, simulate battles in my head or memorize famous orations. If you're someone who likes long discussions on political thought, or history in general, I'd love to talk about it! I also very much stan Julius Caesar.

Travel and Food

I like travelling and car trips. One of my favourite things to do is to simply start up my car and go driving with some friends late at night. I am also quite the foodie and I make sure I get a taste of the cuisine of every place I go, be it a fancy restaurant by the sea in Mumbai or a humble eatery in the hills of Manali. From 5 Stars to Maggie Points, I want to try them all!
